## Uses that are always fine (no permission needed)

- **Nominative / referential use** — truthfully stating that your product,
  article, or service uses, integrates with, is built on, or is compatible with
  DeviceChain (e.g. "powered by DeviceChain", "a DeviceChain plugin").
- **Unmodified redistribution** — distributing official, unmodified DeviceChain
  releases under their original name.
- **Community discussion** — using the name in talks, blog posts, tutorials, and
  documentation that refer to the project.

When you use the Marks this way, please:
1. Use "DeviceChain" as an adjective, not a noun or verb, on first prominent use
   (e.g. "the DeviceChain platform", not "a DeviceChain").
2. Not alter, abbreviate, or combine the Marks with your own marks.
3. Not imply sponsorship, endorsement, or affiliation by IoT Innovations, LLC
   that does not exist.

## Uses that need written permission

- Using the Marks (or confusingly similar names/logos) in the **name of your own
  product, service, company, domain, or app**.
- Distributing a **modified version** of DeviceChain under the DeviceChain name.
  Modified builds must be renamed so users are not confused about the source.
- Using the logo in a way that suggests an official or endorsed distribution.
- Selling merchandise bearing the Marks.

## Requesting permission or reporting misuse

Email **admin@devicechain.io**. We aim to support the community's legitimate use
of the Marks while preventing confusion about the source and quality of
DeviceChain software.
